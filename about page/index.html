About Us

Design & Tech community, FUTO is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting design

and technology within the Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO). We are diverse

group of students from all engineering and computer science disciplines, and we are

passionate about using our skills to make a positive impact on the world.

 

We believe that design and technology have the power to change the world, and we are really

excited to be part of that change. We hope that you will join us on our journey!

Our theme for this year’s conference is Bridging The Gap: AI innovation in Higher Education.

We believe that artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to revolutionize higher education,

and we want to explore how AI can be used to improve teaching, learning, and research.

 

The conference will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, and workshops on a variety

of topics related to AI in higher education. We will also have a poster session where students

can showcase their research on AI.

 

We are excited to bring together a diverse group of students, faculty, and professionals to

discuss the future of AI in higher education. We believe that this conference will provide a

platform for sharing ideas and creating partnerships that will help to shape the future of AI in

educaion.
